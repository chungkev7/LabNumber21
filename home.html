Ypsilanti, Michigan

There are many people that misprounce Ypsilanti (it's pronounced as IP-sil-AN-tee) for those who don't recognize the city. Ypsilanti, located east of Ann Arbor, home of Eastern Michigan University, is an historic city founded in the early 1800s.

Notable people that were from Ypsilanti include Adam Gase (New York Jets head coach), Russell C. Ostrander (former mayor of Lansing and Chief Justice of the Michigan Supreme Court), and Walter Briggs Sr (Detroit Tigers owner from 1919-1952).
